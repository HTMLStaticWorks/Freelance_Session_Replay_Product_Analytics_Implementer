Session Replay Services

Session replays capture real user interactions in video-like playback. I implement secure, compliant tracking so you can watch exactly how visitors navigate your app, locate bugs, and identify friction points.

  • Watch Real Journeys: Stop guessing why users abandon checkout and see their exact struggles.
  • Rage Click & Dead Click Detection: Instantly tag frustrated actions on non-clickable items.
